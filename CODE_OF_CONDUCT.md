# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Treat other contributors with respect.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent until the evidence says otherwise.
- Focus criticism on the code, behavior, or decision, not the person.
- Share context when disagreeing.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Bad-faith disruption of discussions, reviews, or community spaces.

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and any project-managed community spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, and contributions that violate this policy.

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainer through the support channel listed in the repository or App Store listing.
