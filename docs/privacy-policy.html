Privacy Policy for MOBaiLE

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Home · Support

This Privacy Policy explains how MOBaiLE ("the app") processes information when you use the iOS app. MOBaiLE is designed to connect to a backend server that you configure and control.

Information the app processes

  • Connection settings such as server URL and session ID.
  • API token used to authenticate to your backend (stored in iOS Keychain).
  • Text prompts and chat history you submit in the app.
  • Voice recordings when you choose to use microphone input.
  • Photos, files, and other attachments you explicitly choose to import and send to your backend.
  • Run status, logs, and result metadata returned by your backend.

How information is used

The app uses this information only to run your requested tasks, show run progress/results, and persist local app state (for example, your settings and chat threads).

Where information is sent

The iOS app sends prompts, audio, attachments, and related metadata to the backend server URL you configure. If your backend is configured to call third-party providers (for example, AI or transcription services), data may also be processed by those providers under their own policies.

Data sharing and sale

The app does not sell personal data and does not include advertising SDKs or third-party analytics/tracking libraries.

Data retention and deletion

  • On-device settings and chat history remain until you delete them or uninstall the app.
  • Backend-side retention depends on your backend setup and configuration.
  • You can remove app data by uninstalling MOBaiLE and deleting backend data on your server.

Security

You are responsible for securing your backend endpoint. Use HTTPS for non-local connections and manage API tokens carefully.

Children's privacy

MOBaiLE is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated over time. Changes will be published at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions about a distributed build, use the support contact provided with that build or the support channel listed in the app's App Store listing.
