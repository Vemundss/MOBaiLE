MOBaiLE • Public Info

Your computer, in your pocket.

MOBaiLE is an iPhone client for a paired backend running on your own machine. This site hosts the public privacy and support pages used for App Store distribution and links to the open source repository.

Privacy Policy Support Source Code

What It Is

Send a prompt from your phone, run the work on your own Mac or Linux box, and watch progress stream back live in the app.

Privacy Surface

The app stores local settings on-device and sends prompts, audio, attachments, and run metadata to the backend you pair with. Full details are in the privacy policy.

Open Source

The repository, setup scripts, backend, and iOS client are published at github.com/Vemundss/MOBaiLE.

For App Review and Distribution

These public pages exist so the app has stable support and privacy-policy URLs. They should stay reachable, accurate, and in sync with what the shipped app actually does.
