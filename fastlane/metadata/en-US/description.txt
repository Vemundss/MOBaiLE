MOBaiLE is the iPhone control surface for your own Mac or Linux machine.

Start a task from your phone, run it on your paired computer, and keep the whole thread visible while you are away from the keyboard.

What MOBaiLE helps you do:
- Start repo, terminal, and workspace tasks on the computer you already use
- Follow live progress, logs, and final results in one thread
- Send typed requests or quick voice prompts when you cannot stop to type
- Keep separate chats anchored to the right workspace and session context
- Tune backend, runtime, and personal context settings without leaving the app

MOBaiLE is not a cloud IDE and it does not execute code on-device. The phone starts and follows the run. Your Mac or Linux machine does the work. Prompts, audio, attachments, and related metadata are sent only to the backend you choose to pair.
